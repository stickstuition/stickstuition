What is Mastery Learning?

Mastery learning is an educational approach where students are given the time and support needed to fully understand a topic before moving on to the next one. Instead of racing through content, the focus is on achieving a high level of understanding before progressing.

This is where tutoring excels. Unlike traditional classrooms, tutoring offers a personalised pace, tailored explanations, and immediate feedback. These are core elements of mastery learning.

Research by Benjamin Bloom showed that students taught using mastery learning methods can outperform up to 90% of those in standard classrooms. With tutoring, students aren't left behind. They build real understanding, boost confidence, and develop the tools to succeed long-term.
